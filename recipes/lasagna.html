Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingridients

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ⅛ teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the pork, beef, and onion until well browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon parsley, garlic, basil, 1 ½ teaspoons salt, oregano, and sugar. Simmer for about 30 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain.
  5. In a bowl, combine cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, 2 teaspoons salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer with noodles, half of the cottage cheese mixture, half of the mozzarella cheese, and one-third of the remaining meat sauce. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  7. Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
